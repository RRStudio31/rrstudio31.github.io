GeoRank

Step into the world of strategic geographic ranking where knowledge meets puzzle-solving! GeoRank isn't your typical trivia game - it challenges your analytical skills and global awareness.

How to Play:

Presented with a random country, your mission is to strategically rank it across multiple categories. Each category awards points based on the country's global standing (#1 being the best). Your goal? Achieve the lowest total score possible through clever decision-making and strategic thinking.
